COMMUNITY SERVICE

What I Do In Community Service

One of the hardest things to do in the world is to genuinely serve people outside of one's family and close friends without expecting anything in return. I'm talking about volunteering - an act of giving that is essential for keeping humanity alive.

In keeping with that theme, I dedicate my efforts and time to activities that make our society better. Upon graduating, I returned to my hometown of Rubavu to volunteer with Stella Maris Parish, where I regularly attend Mass. We conducted anti-drug campaigns, educational seminars for street children, and fundraising to send them back to school. During my time in school, I also volunteered with Red Cross Rwanda, providing first aid at interschool events.

This past July, my country held presidential elections, and I was excited to volunteer to help the events run safely. Elections matter to me because they determine a leader's vision for the country's development. I was assigned to oversee a section of our sector's polling site, where we worked to explain the voting process, emphasize the importance of elections, and ensure voter safety. I'm glad we were able to make it happen successfully.
