Why I Care About Sports and Digital Well-being

I personally experienced the dark side of social media, including among other things facilitating heightened levels of procrastination and anxiety. Consequently, I’m committed to lowering my digital footprint everyday. I direct myself to associating with the physical world through conversations, hard copies of books, and more.

I exercise everyday not only to gain access to the benefits of it such as improving memory as well as building muscle strength, but also to practice nobel values like commitment, persistence, and consistency– the kind of values that make people move mountains.
