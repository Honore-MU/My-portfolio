What I Do In Art and Photography

Visual arts serve to me as escapes for pressing moments like anger, anxiety, and other inevitable life emotions. I blend acrylics, charcoal, and graphite to bring my imagination to life. When I am drawing, I sketch with varied graphite pencils from 6H to 8B. I use tissue papers to make gradients for backgrounds. Painting helps me capture the vibrant spirit of Rwandan Culture in my artwork as well as expressing emotions through making abstract paintings.

The term photography sounds appealing to me itself let alone the actual process of doing it. I cannot emphasize enough how I feel while adjusting angles and changing modes so as to grab the beauty of nature. It amazes me to see how likely lively trees, stones, flowers, and water bodies appear in my photographs just like in their actual forms.
