Questions and Answers about Me

A compilation of my personal interests about the world. From hardcore rap like “Not Like Us” to admiring the works of great historical figures like Napoleon Bonaparte and Benjamin Franklin, focusing on being a strategist and bold in everything I do. From quirky habits like eating dinner at midnight to mark the end of work, to marveling at the wonders of the world like the Colosseum and the Taj Mahal.

I have an immense love for thriller movies, appreciating the genius of Sheldon Lee Cooper in "Young Sheldon”and the determination of Jonathan Wick in the “John Wick” movies. I’m moved by shows like America’s Got Talent (AGT) and enjoy free swimming lessons at Rocket Swimming, applying them in Lake Kivu.

From philosophical quotes like “He who has a why to live can bear almost any how”by Friedrich Nietzsche to marveling at the discovery of the DNA double helix structure, my favorite scientific symbol. Intense admiration of Intore traditional dance, Rukari Palace Museum, and Lake Kivu.
